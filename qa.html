Section Five

In the Author's Own Words

A Conversation with Ellery Queen

Questions & Answers

Answered in his own voice — unfiltered and straight from the heart.

I want people to know that I am a regular hard working American like most of us out there — nothing special. I just love Jesus Christ our Lord and Savior and thank him every second, every micro second of every day for what he has done — not only for our sins but for the sins of the whole world.

I just started writing one day and haven't stopped since. I believe it is the Holy Spirit working through me to write these spiritual passages and to bring more souls to Jesus Christ our Lord and Savior.

Well that is an easy one — I am a Christian from the word go and that lifestyle and the experiences in this life have inspired me to write this spiritual book. And like I said before, this book is definitely the Holy Ghost working through me.

I would say the Holy Bible — the New Testament, the writings of the Apostles, mainly the Apostle Paul. His writings really inspired me to write this spiritual book.

People ask me why I smile all the time or why I am so happy and I always tell people I am living the life — which means I am living the Christian life to the utmost, every second, every micro second of every day, praising Christ Jesus with every thought of mind and spirit. It is possible. We are not perfect by any means, but we can succeed in Christ in anything that we do every single day.

I was in the Navy — a submariner. I did 11 years and I live a simple life, which is kind of hard to do in this life and time.

This book is a spiritual book — it will take you places spiritually that you could not even imagine you could reach. If you are a believer, a Christian, or not, this book will take you places you have never been before spiritually. It is definitely worth reading if you need guidance in this life, from the good times and the bad. This spiritual book will help you through anything in this life.

The preaching of Jesus Christ and what He did while He was just here a short time in this world is what I try to continue to accomplish in every way — such as loving your neighbor, caring for them that spitefully use you and abuse you, loving your enemies. Even healing — maybe not physically, but with your daily actions. Helping a little old lady across the street, giving money to someone struggling on the street, healing that way and touching every single soul in this life that you can through the Holy Spirit of God the Father and our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.

Well the Holy Ghost inspired me to write this spiritual book — this is what I thank God the Father and Jesus Christ every day for, inspiring me for the writings of this spiritual book. What makes this book different is the passages relate to everyday challenges of this life. You can find a passage that will inspire you in anything you are going through — something as simple as going to the store to get a loaf of bread, to bringing your soul, mind, and spirit to Jesus Christ literally every second of every day.

I really hope if you are not a Christian that you want to become a Christian — and if you are a Christian, you want to strive to be better on your journey as a Christian. Be Christ-like every day. I want to bring as many souls to Christ as I can, and I believe this book can accomplish that.

None that I know of, but this book is a part of my ministry and I have given out hundreds of copies of these spiritual passages — hoping it has brought and saved at least one soul to Jesus Christ our Lord. That is the only recognition I need for this spiritual book: to bring souls to Christ.

That with Jesus Christ, all things are possible through Him — no matter how low you get, no matter how high you get, Jesus Christ our Lord is always there. Even through the worst of times, Jesus will always be there with you until the end of time.

Jesus Christ the Son of God is the only way to gaining everlasting life — He, Christ Jesus, is the way, the truth, and the life.

Well, I am not really working on anything right now but I am still writing passages — hopefully for a book in the near future.

I feel as though I stay in touch through the writings of this book — Spiritual Passages — and that will take us beyond where we need to go spiritually.

Well, I start out the day every day on my knees, thanking our Lord and Savior for what He did — not only for our sins but for the sins of the whole world. Not only dying on the cross and shedding His precious blood, but going beyond the cross, having His precious soul suffer in hell for our sins. I also pray for forgiveness of my sins. I live this every second, every micro second of every day. This inspires me to do well during my life in this world and helps me to make it through every single day.

Ellery Queen

"Jesus Christ is the way, the truth, and the life." — John 14:6
