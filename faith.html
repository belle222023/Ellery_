Section Three

Faith & Mission

The Mission

This book is a part of my ministry. I have given out hundreds of copies of these spiritual passages and I hope it has brought at least one soul to Jesus Christ our Lord. That is the only recognition I need — to bring souls to Christ.

I want to bring as many souls to Christ as I can, and I believe this book can accomplish that.

What Writing Has Taught Me

"That with Jesus Christ, all things are possible through Him — no matter how low you get, no matter how high you get, Jesus Christ our Lord is always there. Even through the worst of times, Jesus will always be there with you until the end of time."

The Daily Practice

I start out the day every day on my knees, thanking our Lord and Savior for what He did — not only for our sins but for the sins of the whole world. Not only dying on the cross and shedding His precious blood, but going beyond the cross, having His precious soul suffer in hell for our sins. I also pray for forgiveness of my sins. I live this every second, every micro second of every day.

Living with Joy

People ask me why I smile all the time or why I am so happy. I am living the Christian life to the utmost, every second, every micro second of every day, praising Christ Jesus with every thought of mind and spirit. It is possible — we are not perfect by any means, but we can succeed in Christ in anything that we do every single day.

Inspired by the Apostles

I would say the Holy Bible — the New Testament, the writings of the Apostles, mainly the Apostle Paul. His writings really inspired me to write this spiritual book. I am a Christian from the word go — and that lifestyle and the experiences in this life have inspired me to write these passages.

Jesus Christ the Son of God is the only way to gaining everlasting life.

He is the way, the truth, and the life. — John 14:6
