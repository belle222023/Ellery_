The Writing Life

Process & Practice

How the Spirit Moves

How It Began

I just started writing one day and haven't stopped since. I believe it is the Holy Spirit working through me to write these spiritual passages and to bring more souls to Jesus Christ our Lord and Savior.

A Typical Writing Day

I start out the day every day on my knees, thanking our Lord and Savior for what He did — not only for our sins but for the sins of the whole world. Not only dying on the cross and shedding His precious blood, but going beyond the cross, having His precious soul suffer in hell for our sins. I also pray for forgiveness of my sins. I live this every second, every micro second of every day. This inspires me to do well during my life in this world and helps me to make it through every single day.

Coming Soon

I am not really working on a new book right now, but I am still writing passages — hopefully for a book in the near future. The Spirit continues to move, and the words keep coming. Stay connected through the words already given.

Staying Connected

I feel as though I stay in touch through the writings of this book — these spiritual passages — and that will take us beyond where we need to go spiritually. The book itself is the connection between the author and the reader.

"I am a Christian from the word go — and that lifestyle and the experiences in this life have inspired me to write this spiritual book. This book is definitely the Holy Ghost working through me."

The Source of Inspiration

The Holy Bible — the New Testament, the writings of the Apostles, mainly the Apostle Paul. His writings really inspired me to write this spiritual book. The Word of God is alive and active.

The Purpose Behind Every Word

With Jesus Christ, all things are possible through Him — no matter how low you get, no matter how high you get, Jesus Christ our Lord is always there. That truth drives every passage I write.
