Living the Life

People ask me why I smile all the time or why I am so happy. I always tell them I am living the life — the Christian life to the utmost, every second of every day, praising Christ Jesus with every thought of mind and spirit. It is possible. We are not perfect by any means, but we can succeed in Christ in anything that we do every single day.
