Spiritual Passages

This book is a spiritual book. It will take you places spiritually that you could not even imagine you could reach. If you are a believer, a Christian, or not — this book will take you places you have never been before spiritually. It is definitely worth reading if you need guidance in this life, from the good times and the bad. This spiritual book will help you through anything in this life.

What Makes This Book Different

The passages relate to everyday challenges of this life. You can find a passage that will inspire you in anything you are going through — something as simple as going to the store to get a loaf of bread, to bringing your soul, mind, and spirit to Jesus Christ literally every second of every day.

The Holy Ghost Working Through Me

The Holy Ghost inspired me to write this spiritual book. This is what I thank God the Father and Jesus Christ every day for — inspiring me in the writings of this spiritual book. Well what makes this book different is the passages relate to everyday challenges of this life.

The Most Important Lesson

That with Jesus Christ all things are possible through Him — no matter how low you get, no matter how high you get, Jesus Christ our Lord is always there. Even through the worst of times, Jesus will always be there with you until the end of time.

"I really hope if you are not a Christian that you want to become a Christian — and if you are a Christian, you want to strive to be better on your journey. Be Christ-like every day."

Themes & Human Experiences

🕊

The preaching of Jesus Christ and what He accomplished in His time in this world — continuing to carry that mission forward in every way

❤

Loving your neighbor and caring for those who spitefully use and abuse you — loving your enemies without condition

🙏

Healing not physically but through your daily actions — touching every single soul through the Holy Spirit of God the Father

✨

Helping the struggling, giving to those in need, and serving through the spirit of God the Father and our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ

Recognition & Ministry

None that I know of, but this book is a part of my ministry and I have given out hundreds of copies of these spiritual passages — hoping it has brought and saved at least one soul to Jesus Christ our Lord. That is the only recognition I need for this spiritual book: to bring souls to Christ.
